Our Story: More Than Just a Café

The Spice Route was founded in 2018 with a simple vision: to create a space where the rich aroma of books and the comforting warmth of a perfect brew intertwine. We're not just a bookstore or a café; we're a community hub for Jalandhar's literary lovers and creative minds.

"Every cup holds a story, and every book, a journey." - Simran, Owner.
